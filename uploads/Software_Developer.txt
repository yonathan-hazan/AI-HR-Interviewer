Job Title: Software Developer
Company: Amazon
Location: Seattle, WA

About Amazon:
Amazon is a global leader in e-commerce and cloud computing, known for its customer-centric approach and innovative technologies. We're looking for talented software developers to join our team and help shape the future of our products and services.

Job Description:
As a Software Developer at Amazon, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining scalable software solutions that power our global e-commerce platform and cloud services. You'll work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, tackling complex problems and contributing to cutting-edge technologies.

Key Responsibilities:
1. Design and implement scalable, efficient, and reliable software solutions
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features
3. Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code
4. Participate in code reviews and contribute to engineering best practices
5. Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize software performance
6. Stay up-to-date with emerging software development technologies and trends

Requirements:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
2. 3+ years of professional software development experience
3. Strong proficiency in one or more programming languages such as Java, Python, C++, or Go
4. Experience with distributed systems and cloud computing platforms (AWS experience is a plus)
5. Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and software design principles
6. Familiarity with agile development methodologies
7. Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
8. Strong communication and teamwork abilities

Preferred Qualifications:
1. Experience with machine learning or AI technologies
2. Contributions to open-source projects
3. Knowledge of microservices architecture and containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes)
4. Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark)

What We Offer:
1. Competitive salary and benefits package
2. Opportunities for career growth and development
3. Chance to work on impactful projects that serve millions of customers worldwide
4. Collaborative and innovative work environment
5. Employee discount on Amazon products and services

Amazon is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive environment for all employees.
