Job Title: Data Analyst at Google

Location: Ramat Gan, Israel

Job Description:
Google is seeking a talented and motivated Data Analyst to join our team in Tel Aviv. The successful candidate will be responsible for collecting, processing, and performing statistical analysis of data. You'll work closely with various teams to provide insights that drive business decisions and improve our products.

Key Responsibilities:
- Collect and analyze large datasets using SQL and other data query tools
- Create and maintain dashboards to visualize key metrics
- Develop and implement data collection systems and other strategies
- Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
- Work with stakeholders to identify and define new measurement opportunities

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in a data analyst role
- Strong proficiency in SQL and experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark)
- Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker)
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Fluency in Python or R for data analysis and manipulation
- Strong communication skills in English; Hebrew is a plus
- Familiarity with Google Cloud Platform is preferred
- Experience with machine learning techniques is a bonus

Skills:
- Data analysis
- Statistical modeling
- SQL
- Python or R
- Data visualization
- Problem-solving
- Communication
